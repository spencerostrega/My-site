The Hot Rod Experience Launch

Strategic overhaul and production for a dedicated classic car and track enthusiast brand.

1M+

Views in 6 Months

Strategy & Execution

Operating as the Lead Growth Strategist for The Hot Rod Experience, I was tasked with modernizing a brand that had plateaued due to stagnant, legacy content formats. The objective was to fundamentally redesign its digital DNA to become a high-performance, engaging content engine. My first step was a comprehensive audit, followed by a decisive pivot toward high-impact, personal, short-form video. I shifted the focus to the visceral elements of car culture—race days, track performance, and sponsor highlights—packaged with a fast-paced, highly authentic touch optimized for modern platform algorithms. By rigorously managing daily production and establishing a feedback loop based on viewer data, we tapped into a younger demographic while re-engaging the core enthusiast base. The results were immediate: within the first six months, we surpassed the 1 million view mark, proving that dormant automotive brands can achieve hyper-growth with disciplined strategic execution.

TallTechChannel & E-commerce Integration

Scaling a technical audience and converting engagement into direct sales.

500+

Orders Processed

Strategy & Execution

TallTechChannel began as a disciplined digital experiment: publishing a high-quality, technical video every single day for 100 consecutive days. In the detail-oriented niche of 3D printing, consistently delivering value through time-lapses, troubleshooting, and guides established immense brand authority. The channel quickly grew to nearly 5,000 highly engaged subscribers. Capitalizing on this earned trust, I launched an integrated e-commerce operation providing specialized 3D printing accessories. Because the audience relied on the technical expertise demonstrated in the content, conversion rates significantly outperformed standard industry benchmarks. This project serves as a blueprint for how consistent, value-driven content builds a loyal customer base ready to purchase.

Spence Sells Cars: Dealership Traffic

Driving localized, organic foot traffic directly to the showroom floor.

$100k+

Direct Sales Attributed

Strategy & Execution

While working the sales floor specializing in Chevrolet and GM vehicles, I recognized that traditional, expensive dealership ad spend was yielding diminishing returns. "Spence Sells Cars" was developed to prove that authentic, personality-driven content could significantly outperform paid regional ads. By creating entertaining, informative content that demystified the car buying process and highlighted inventory dynamically, the channel quickly gained traction. Over an eight-month period, the content generated over 5 million views organically. More importantly, these views translated directly to the bottom line. Customers actively sought me out at the dealership based on the content, bypassing standard lead funnels. This resulted in over $100,000 in direct, verifiable sales revenue—demonstrating my ability to not just generate digital impressions, but to drive physical, paying customers through automotive retail doors.
