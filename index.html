The Hot Rod Experience Launch

Strategy and production for a classic car enthusiast brand.

1M+

Views in 6 Months

Case Summary

When I first engaged with The Hot Rod Experience, the brand was not in the best shape. It had suffered through stagnant years of sporadic posting, resulting in a plateaued follower count and minimal organic engagement. The content that was being produced lacked cohesion, failed to adapt to new platform standards, and was effectively being ignored by the relevant algorithms. The challenge was not just to revive it, but to fundamentally redesign its digital DNA to become a high-performance content engine. The first step was a complete strategic audit of all existing assets and a decisive pivot toward what the current platforms favor: high-impact, personal, short-form video optimized vertically for TikTok and Instagram Reels. This involved moving away from passive, quote-driven content to a rigorous, daily production schedule focused on the visceral elements of car culture: race days, and sponsor highlights, with a fast paced and personal touch. This shift was the catalyst. By meticulously crafting content that was both authentic to the brand and engineered for virality, we tapped into a new, younger demographic while simultaneously re-engaging the core enthusiast base. We leveraged trending audio, refined our posting times, and created a feedback loop based on viewer data. The results were immediate and explosive. Within the first six months, we didn't just wake up the audience; we expanded it exponentially, achieving a verifiable surge that surpassed the 1 million view mark for the first time in the brand's history. This revitalization proved that even dormant brands can be resurrected and propelled into hyper-growth with the right strategic content lever and production discipline.

TallTechChannel E-commerce Integration

Built and scaled an online shop specializing in 3D printing accessories.

500+

Orders Processed

Case Summary

TallTechChannel started as a personal challenge and a digital experiment: What happens when you commit to posting a high-quality video every single day for 100 days straight? In the niche world of 3D printing, where technical detail often slows down production, this was a bold move. The strategy relied on volume and value. By flooding the feed with helpful tips, time-lapses, and troubleshooting guides, I capitalized on the platform's need for consistent content. The discipline required to shoot, edit, and post daily was immense, but the data spoke for itself. The channel grew rapidly, amassing nearly 5,000 subscribers who weren't just casual viewers—they were makers looking for solutions. This trust translated directly into commerce. I launched an integrated e-commerce shop selling 3D printing accessories, and the conversion rates were significantly higher than industry standards. The "100 Days" experiment proved that consistency builds trust, and trust builds a loyal customer base.

Spence Sells Cars Viral Content

Rapid deployment of high-performing, niche automotive content.

5M+

Recent Views

Case Summary

In the highly competitive automotive sales market, most dealerships rely on heavy ad spend. "Spence Sells Cars" was built on a different premise: pure organic reach. I wanted to prove that personal branding and authentic content could outsell paid ads. I focused on personality-driven content that demystified the car buying process and showcased inventory in an entertaining light. By staying on top of trending audio and video formats, the channel hit a viral vein. In just eight months, the content generated over 5 million views across platforms, all without spending a dollar on promotion. Crucially, these weren't just vanity metrics. The visibility created a sales funnel that led directly to the dealership floor. Customers walked in holding their phones, asking for "the guy from TikTok." This organic traffic resulted in over $100,000 in direct sales revenue, validating the power of a personal brand in a traditional retail environment.
